Our paper is accepted to ACM Conference on Human Factors in Computing Systems (CHI-26) 🎉.

<br><br>
This paper introduces <b>FutureMe</b>, a generative AI–driven system that helps children imagine self-represented future selves through photo-based, step-by-step narrative creation, fostering continuity between their present experiences and future aspirations.
