Our paper is accepted to Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR-26) 🎉.

<br><br>
This paper introduces <b>CREward</b>, a type-specific reward model that evaluates and guides image creativity across three distinct axes—geometry, material, and texture—by leveraging Large Vision-Language Models (LVLMs) aligned with human perception.
